Installing KSU Compus

To install KSU Compus on your iPhone follow the steps provided below:

  1. Open this website in Safari if you are using a different browser.
  2. Open the share menu by pressing the button at the bottom center of the browser navigation bar.
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the share menu and select the "Add to Home Screen" option.
  4. When the "Add to Home Screen" popup menu appears, press the "Add" button in the top right corner.
